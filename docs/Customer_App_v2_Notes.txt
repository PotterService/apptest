POTTER SERVICE CUSTOMER APP V2

Public domain: app.potterservice.com
No guest requests. All users must have accounts.

Included prototype ideas:
- Welcome using the logged-in name; preview name is Sparkles.
- Bow online/offline status and Facebook contact button when offline.
- Visible starting rates before ride/delivery request.
- Customer notifications after quote, acceptance, on-the-way, arrival, and completion.
- Payment choices: PayPal, cash on pickup, card on pickup, and account charge when approved.
- In-app messaging after acceptance through drop-off/completion.
- Rewards page and home redeem-code box.
- Discount codes, Wait & Ride credits, Pickup Backups, and stackable code counts.
- Pickup Backup setup/request flow, expiration, confirmation warning, and forfeiture after acceptance.
- Account balances and optional negative limits controlled by Bow.
- User selects account charge before submitting; Bow sees balance and limit.
- Admin-created requests appear on the customer's Status page.
- Event Day mode with event address locked, to/from selection, event queue, starting rate, included miles, and overage pricing.
- Business accounts can log in while pending but cannot request until approved.
- Temporary event-manager accounts and date restrictions.
- Account blacklist, suspension, and temporary restriction controls planned for the private app.

This is a visual prototype only. No real payments, notifications, messages, accounts, or database are connected.
